Dan Costello

Dan is the tallest baritone in Downtown Crossing. He also directs The Vocal Revolution (DTX's reluctant home-chapter).

About Us

Downtown Crossing is the 2013 Northeastern District Champion, but you're just as likely to hear us singing on the T as on stage. All our friends call us DTX, so you should too! Much to Nashville's dismay, Downtown Crossing is an officially registered quartet of the Barbershop Harmony Society.
